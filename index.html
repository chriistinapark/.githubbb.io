What we do

Listening to your stories
Solving problems & seeing opportunities
Realising potential

Listening carefully to clients and building users helps to identify
the unique set of requirements for each project. Combining this
with in depth research about the local context and
sustainability enables a clear brief to be agreed.

Considered, thoughtful architecture can promote a
sense of wellbeing, social value and community
cohesion. PCA are therefore committed to giving equal
weight to intelligent, people centred design that
improves the quality of life for its users as well as the
function, cost and build ability requirements of the
building.

Once the brief is established, we work hard to find
creative solutions to the unique requirements of each
project. Every project is different, and we give an
individually tailored response to suit the specific needs
of your brief. Communicating our ideas clearly and
simply and explaining the process as we move through
the project is essential.
